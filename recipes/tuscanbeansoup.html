Tuscan Bean Soup

Description:

A simple yet delicious soup that can be enjoyed year round. Good source of protein.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 chopped carrots
  • 2 chopped celery stocks
  • 4 crushed garlic cloves
  • 2 sliced zucchini
  • 4 (14 ounce) cans of vegetable stock
  • 1 (15 ounce) can of cannellini beans
  • 1 (14 ounce) can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup pesto
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) bag of fresh spinach

Steps:

  1. Heat 1/4 cup olive oil in a large stockpot over medium heat
  2. Saute onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until lightly browned (about 10 minutes)
  3. Add zucchini and cook until slightly soft (3 to 4 minutes)
  4. Stir vegetable stock, cannellini beans, chopped tomatoes, chicken stock, and pesto into vegetable mixture, and bring to a boil
  5. Reduce temperature to medium-low heat and simmer until vegetables are tender (30 to 35 minutes)
  6. Salt and pepper
  7. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  8. Saute spinach until wilted (about 5 minutes)
  9. Spoon sauteed spinach into each serving bowl
