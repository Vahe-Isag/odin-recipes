Basted Eggs

Description:

This delicious yet simple recipe will have you create a flavorful and less fussy version of a poached egg on top of toasted bread.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 tablespoonts of salted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinchy flaky sea salt, or to taste

Steps:

  1. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Crack eggs into the skillet and cook until whites have nearly set (about 1 minute)
  3. Add water, cover the skillet, and reduce heat output to medium-low
  4. Let it cook until whites have set and the yolk is runny (1 to 2 minutes)
  5. Remove the cover and transfer the eggs onto a piece of toast.
  6. Garnish with pepper and salt if desired.
