Chicken Pot Pie

Description:

Chicken pot pie made from scratch with carrots, peas, and celery with a crunchy crust.

Ingredients:

  • Boneless, skinless chicken
  • Peas, carrots, onion, celery, mushrooms, and green peppers.
  • Salt, pepper, and celery seed.
  • Chicken broth and milk for smoothness and consistency
  • Two unbaked pie crust

Steps:

  1. Boil the cubed chicken along with the beggies for 15 minutes then drain and set them aside.
  2. Cook the onions in butter until translucent, then stir in the flour and seasonings.
  3. Add the chicken broth and milk and simmer until the filling is thick.
  4. Place the chicken and vegetable mixture into an unbaked pie crust. Pour the chicken broth and milk mixture over it.
  5. Cover the initial crust with the second crust and seal the edges.
  6. Cut slits in the tiop to allow the steam to escape.
  7. Bake until the pastry is golden brown and filling bubbly.
